I Ruff You is an adorable/ hilarious dog dating sim created during the spare time of a volunteer and a participant during a game jam.

Explore a cute lil dog park and romance your way into the hearts of any of the four eligible bachelors!

We spent about 8 hours to create it during a 48 hour game jam, and will adamantly use this ridiculously short time frame to excuse all of its flaws.
